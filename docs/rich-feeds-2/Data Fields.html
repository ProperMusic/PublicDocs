Additional Fields​

On request, Proper Music can add additional fields to an individual feed. If you would like additional specific data, then please contact your label manager or other Proper Music contact who can request additional fields, for copyright and data protection reasons, not all the data held by Proper Music can be made available in a feed.

Naming and format of any Additional Fields will be communicated when they are configured,
